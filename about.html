Riya Gandhi


Web Developer & Engineer


Riya Gandhi is a Bachelor of Computer Science graduate from New Jersey City University, with a strong technical background in web development and software troubleshooting. Her expertise lies in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and SQL. She has gained practical experience in various roles, including Quality Control Associate at Blink, North Reading, MA, and System Test Technician at ZT Systems, Secaucus, NJ.


During her tenure as a Quality Control Associate at Blink, Riya was responsible for conducting manual testing of the functions and features of Blink devices, analyzing test results, and reporting issues to the team lead. She worked collaboratively with engineering to ensure high-quality products were delivered to customers. Her experience as a System Test Technician at ZT Systems involved repairing and troubleshooting servers, managing hardware and software, analyzing tests, and working collaboratively with team leads and supervisors.


Riya has also completed training courses to gain certifications, such as CompTIA A+ from NPower New Jersey and web development training from InternShala. As a part of her coursework, she designed and developed e-commerce websites, created scripts for time management, and maintained various university databases.


Overall, Riya's strong technical skills, attention to detail, and ability to work collaboratively make her a valuable asset to any team.
